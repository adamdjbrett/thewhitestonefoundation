About Us

The Whitestone Foundations dba Whitestone Publications is a Colorado-registered 501©3 non-profit corporation.

It publishes two professional e-journals: The New Polis and The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory, which are open access journals. It also sponsors webinars and seminars including Critical Conversations and Difficult Discussions.

Whitestone Publications is engaged collaboratively with the University of Denver and the South Dakota State University.

The Whitestone Foundations dba Whitestone Publications is a Colorado-registered 501(c)3 non-profit corporation.  It publishes two professional e-journals: *[The New Polis](http://www.thenewpolis.com)*, *[The Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory](http://www.jcrt.org)*, which are open access journals. It also sponsors webinars and seminars including *Critical Conversations* and *Difficult Discussions*. 

Whitestone Publications is engaged collaboratively with the University of Denver and the South Dakota State University.

Whitestone Publications does not retain copyright to its publications. It automatically grants copyright and rights to republication to all authors in either blog or journal format, so long as the original entry is cited in any instance of republication.  Example:

##### This article was originally published as Smith, J.D., “Theory and Method in the Global Study of Religion”,*Journal for Cultural and Religious Theory* 12 (Spring 2011):248-68.
